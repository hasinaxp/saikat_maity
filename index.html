About Me

I graduated from the Indian College of Art & Draftsmanship in Drawing & Painting with First Class in 2007 & have won the Best Watercolour Award from the Academy of Fine Arts & another from the Camlin Art Foundation. In watercolours, the beauty lies in the freshness of colours, their transparency & purity, & a slightly unfinished look , resulting in a natural effect. My watercolours are dramatic and realistic, infusing life in even commonplace objects & making them look spectacular !
